For Part II, my goal was to recreate a police siren.

I did this using a sine wave with frequency 700Hz, and applying frequency modulation using an LFO. The LFO has an amplitude of 300, sweeping the signal from 400Hz to 1000Hz. Its frequency can be toggled between 0.3Hz for a slow sweep and 3Hz for a fast one.

Additionally, I incorporated a bandpass filter centered at 1500Hz to amplify the higher frequencies. This was done in efforts to simulate the car horn through which a police siren would play.
